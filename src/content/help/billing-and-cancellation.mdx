# Billing and cancellation

SiteSync is priced flat. No per-seat fees, no per-project fees, no module unlocks.

- **$400/month** billed monthly, or
- **$4,080/year** billed annually (15% off — save $720/year)

Both plans include unlimited users, unlimited projects, all modules, IRIS AI, the full audit pack, and SSO. The only thing gated is usage volume (storage, AI tokens) at thresholds far above what a normal GC will hit.

## The 14-day trial

Sign up at `sitesync.app` with an email — no credit card required to start. The trial includes:

- Full access to every feature for 14 days.
- Up to 3 projects, unlimited users, 25 GB storage, 1M IRIS tokens.
- A founder-onboarding offer: book a 30-minute setup call with Walker during your trial.

### Adding payment to continue past day 14

On **day 11** of trial, SiteSync prompts you in-app and via email to add a payment method. You can also navigate to **Settings → Billing → Add Payment Method** at any time.

We use Stripe. Cards, ACH (US bank), and SEPA (EU) are supported. Add the method, pick monthly or annual, and your subscription activates at trial end.

If trial ends without a payment method on file, your account enters **read-only mode** (see below). Data is preserved; you just can't write to it until billing is resolved.

## Payment failures and dunning

If a charge fails (expired card, insufficient funds, fraud-block), SiteSync retries on the following schedule:

| Attempt | Day | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Day 0 (charge date) | Initial charge. |
| 2 | Day 3 | Retry. Email to owner + admin. |
| 3 | Day 7 | Retry. In-app banner. Email + Crisp chat ping. |
| 4 | Day 11 | Retry. Banner escalates to red. Last warning before read-only. |
| 5 | Day 14 | Final retry. If still failing, account enters read-only mode. |

After 14 days of failed retries, the account flips to **read-only**. No further automated retries — you'll need to update the payment method manually.

## Read-only mode

When read-only mode triggers (trial expired without payment, or 14 days of failed dunning):

- All users can **read** every page — projects, RFIs, drawings, daily logs, audit trail, exports.
- No user can **write** — no new RFIs, no daily log entries, no schedule edits, no IRIS drafts.
- A red banner appears at the top of every page: *"Read-only mode. Update payment to resume."*
- The owner gets daily email reminders for 30 days.

To exit read-only: **Settings → Billing → Update Payment Method**. As soon as a charge succeeds, write access is restored within 60 seconds.

## How to cancel

1. **Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription**.
2. Confirm by typing your org name.
3. Pick effective date: **End of current billing period** (default — you keep access until then) or **Immediate** (no refund).
4. Optional: tell us why. This goes directly to Walker.

Cancellation is self-serve. No retention call, no friction, no need to email support.

## What happens to your data after cancel

- **Immediately:** Account flips to read-only. All users can log in and export but cannot write.
- **Day 0 to 90:** Soft-delete window. Re-subscribe at any time to restore full access with zero data loss.
- **Day 90:** Hard delete. Project data, photos, drawings, and PII are permanently scrubbed from the production database and storage.
- **Forever:** The hash-chained audit trail summary (event counts, timestamps, anonymized actors) is retained for 7 years per US construction record-keeping convention. No content, no PII — just enough to attest the chain existed and wasn't tampered with.

## Data export before cancel

We make leaving easy. Before you cancel — or anytime during the 90-day soft-delete window — export your data:

- **Settings → Billing → Export All Data** generates a single ZIP with:
  - All projects as JSON (RFIs, submittals, daily logs, schedule, budget).
  - All drawings as PDF.
  - All photos in original resolution.
  - All documents in original format.
  - The full hash-chained audit log as JSON + verification appendix PDF.

Export usually completes in 15–60 minutes depending on storage volume. We email you when it's ready. Link expires in 7 days but you can regenerate.

## Refunds

- **Monthly:** No refunds for the current month. Cancellation takes effect at period end.
- **Annual:** Pro-rated refund of unused months minus a 15% admin fee. Email `walker@sitesync.app` to request.
- **Within 30 days of first paid charge:** Full refund, no questions asked. Email Walker.
