# Using SiteSync on your phone (PWA install)

SiteSync is a Progressive Web App. Install it from your phone's browser to get an icon on the home screen, no app-store wait, no separate download.

## iOS (Safari)

1. Open SiteSync in Safari (`https://app.sitesyncai.com`)
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with up arrow)
3. Scroll to **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

## Android (Chrome)

1. Open SiteSync in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots) → **Install app**
3. Confirm

## Why PWA over native

- Same codebase as the web app — every fix lands everywhere
- No Apple / Google app-store delay
- Push notifications, camera access, offline mode all supported
- Native iOS app coming in Q3 2026 for App Store visibility
