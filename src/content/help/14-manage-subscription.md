# Managing your subscription

Settings → Billing shows your current plan, trial status, next invoice, and payment history.

## Plans

SiteSync Pro is the single self-serve tier:

- **$400 / month**
- **$4,080 / year** (15% off — billed once)
- Unlimited projects, unlimited users
- AI Copilot included
- 100 GB storage

Enterprise plans are custom — email **walker@sitesyncai.com**.

## Switching monthly ↔ annual

Use the **Change plan** button. Stripe prorates the change automatically.

## Trial

New accounts get a 14-day trial with card on file. We charge on day 14; you can cancel anytime before then with no charge.
