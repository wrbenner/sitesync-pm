# Creating a submittal

A submittal is the way the GC asks the architect to approve a product, finish, or shop drawing.

## Quick path

1. **Submittals** → **New submittal**.
2. Pick the spec section (CSI code).
3. Upload the product data, shop drawing, or sample photo.
4. Iris suggests the reviewer and a due date.

## Stamping

When the architect responds, they can stamp **approved**, **approved as noted**, **revise and resubmit**, or **rejected**. The stamp + comments are captured on the audit chain.

## Resubmittals

Revise-and-resubmit creates a child submittal linked to the parent so the history is intact.
