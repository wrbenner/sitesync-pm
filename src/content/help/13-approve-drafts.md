# Approving and rejecting AI drafts

Every Iris draft requires explicit approval before it goes anywhere. This is by design — the AI doesn't get to send for you.

## Approve

Single-click in the AI panel. The draft becomes the official record; the audit chain notes who approved it and when.

## Reject

Reject + a one-line reason. The reason feeds back to the voice linter so future drafts on similar inputs avoid the same problem.

## Request clarification

If the draft is *almost* right, edit it inline and approve the edited version. The original AI version is preserved in the audit log.

## Auto-withdraw

Drafts not acted on within 7 days auto-withdraw to keep the inbox clean. They're recoverable from the audit log; nothing is ever truly deleted.
