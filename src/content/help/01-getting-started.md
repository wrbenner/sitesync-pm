# Getting started in 5 minutes

SiteSync is built around the **Nine** — the nine workflows every construction project needs: RFIs, submittals, daily logs, punch items, budget, change orders, schedule, drawings, and closeout.

Here's how to get from sign-in to your first AI-drafted RFI in 5 minutes.

## 1. Create your project

After sign-up, the onboarding wizard prompts for your first project. Name, address, contract value, and dates. You can use sample data if you'd rather poke around before committing real numbers.

## 2. Invite your team

Add up to 5 teammates by email. Each gets a role (owner, admin, member) — see [roles and permissions](/help/roles-permissions) for what each can do.

## 3. Try Iris, the AI Copilot

The AI panel suggests drafts for RFIs, submittals, and daily logs based on context (drawings, photos, prior RFIs). Every draft is tied to a hash-chained audit log so you can prove what was generated when. See [What is the AI Copilot?](/help/iris-copilot).
