# Inviting your team

Settings → Team. Enter email addresses, pick a role per invite, send. Invitees receive a branded email with a one-click sign-up link.

## Roles at invite time

- **Owner** — full control, can delete the workspace
- **Admin** — manage projects + team, cannot delete the workspace
- **Member** — work on assigned projects; can create RFIs, submittals, etc.

## Pending invites

Pending invites show on the Team page. You can resend or revoke at any time. Invites expire in 7 days.
