# Creating an RFI

An RFI (Request for Information) is the formal way to ask the design team a question that affects construction.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **RFIs** → **New RFI**.
2. Pick the trade and CSI section if applicable.
3. Drop or paste any drawings, photos, or PDFs.
4. Iris will draft the body — review, edit, then submit.

## What Iris does for you

- Looks at attached drawings + photos
- Suggests the right answerer based on title block + RFI history
- Suggests a due date based on schedule slack
- Estimates impact ($ and days) from similar past RFIs

You stay in control. Iris drafts; you approve.
