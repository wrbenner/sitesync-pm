# Canceling your subscription

Settings → Billing → **Cancel subscription**. We ask one question (why are you canceling?) before sending you to the Stripe portal to confirm.

## What happens

- Cancel takes effect at the end of the current billing period — no proration credit, no surprise charge.
- Your data stays accessible for 60 days after the period ends.
- After 60 days, your data is soft-deleted. Recoverable for 30 more days on request, then permanently deleted.

## Annual prepay

Annual prepays are non-refundable but cancel-at-period-end. You retain access through the end of the prepaid year.

## Export before canceling

Settings → Workspace → **Export all data** generates a ZIP of every record in your workspace. Always export before you cancel.
