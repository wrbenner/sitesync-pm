SiteSync PM is the modern construction project management platform built for general contractors who care about the field. It combines a real construction PM toolkit with deep AI assistance — without forcing you to swap out the integrations and processes you already trust.

## What's in the box

- **RFIs** — drafting, AI-assisted draft, distribution, ball-in-court tracking, ARC reply ingestion.
- **Submittals** — CSI-aware spec parser, AIA-style approval routing, transmittal logs, attachment management.
- **Change orders** — PCO → COR → CO promotion lifecycle with budget rollups and approval gates.
- **Daily logs** — voice capture, weather pull, manpower roll-up, signature export, instant PDFs.
- **Punch list** — Kanban + drawing-pin views, photo annotation, sub-completion verification.
- **Drawings** — deep-zoom tile viewer, AI title-block extraction, version overlays, markup with collaborative cursors.
- **Schedule** — PDF/MS Project import, critical-path engine, baseline tracking, health metrics.
- **Pay applications** — AIA G702/G703 PDFs, retainage hold/release, Stripe Connect payouts.
- **Compliance** — Davis-Bacon prevailing wage validation, WH-347 certified payroll generation.

## What makes us different

1. **AI you can trust** — Anthropic Claude is wired into RFI drafting, drawing classification, schedule extraction, and a project-aware copilot. Every AI action is traceable in the audit log.
2. **Field-first mobile** — installable PWA + Capacitor wrappers for iOS/Android. Offline queue for jobsite spots without coverage. 56px touch targets for gloved use.
3. **Real integrations** — Procore (RFI + submittal sync) and Sage 300 (cost code + invoice sync) are first-class. Primavera P6 `.xer` import works out of the box.
4. **Multi-tenant by design** — every row is RLS-scoped to project + organization. Tenant isolation is enforced in the database, not the application.
