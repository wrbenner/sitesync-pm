Signup at SiteSync is intentionally fast. The goal is that you're clicking around real-feeling project data within a minute of arriving.

## What you'll get

When you create a new organization, SiteSync immediately seeds it with the **"Maple Ridge Mixed-Use"** demo project — a realistic mid-construction storyline with:

- 30 RFIs in various states (closed, in review, open)
- 12 submittals across CSI sections
- 6 change orders (PCO, COR, CO mix)
- 60 punch items spread across trades and floors
- 14 days of daily logs with weather and crew data
- 5 drawings, 12 schedule phases, 8 vendors

You can click around, edit anything, run the AI copilot against the data, generate PDFs, etc. It's a real Supabase project — just isolated to your org and read-only-via-convention so you don't have to worry about breaking anything.

When you want to start clean for a real customer walkthrough, hit **Reset Demo** in Project Settings and the data springs back to its starting state.

## How to sign up

1. Visit [sitesync-pm.vercel.app/signup](https://sitesync-pm.vercel.app/signup).
2. Enter your email, password, and full name.
3. Click **Create account**.
4. (Optional but recommended) Enable two-factor authentication from your profile — see [Two-factor authentication](../trust/two-factor-authentication).

Your dashboard will load straight into the demo project. Use the project switcher in the top-left to create a real project when you're ready.

## What happens behind the scenes

Three things spin up in the background when you sign up:

1. **Auth user** — managed by Supabase Auth. Password hashed via bcrypt; email verification optional per org policy.
2. **Organization** — created with you as the owner. Every row in your tenant carries this `organization_id`.
3. **Demo project seeding** — `seedDemoProject(orgId)` inserts the Maple Ridge fixture across 8 tables (projects, schedule_phases, rfis, submittals, change_orders, punch_items, daily_logs, drawings). This is fail-soft: a partial seed still leaves you with a usable dashboard.

## Common issues

**"This email is already registered."**
You probably have an account from a previous trial. Use [sign in](https://sitesync-pm.vercel.app/login) or reset your password.

**"Network error."**
Often a corporate VPN blocking Supabase's API endpoints. Disable the VPN momentarily, or have your IT team allowlist `*.supabase.co`.

**"Demo project didn't load."**
Refresh once. The seeder runs async after org creation. If the issue persists, contact `support@sitesync.app` with your org name — the seeder logs partial errors to the browser console.
