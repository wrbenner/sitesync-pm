## TL;DR

This is a **founder-led seed pitch** for a category-defining bet: AI superintendent + free sub portal + embedded payments + hash-chain audit = the next Procore. The deck has to hit four notes simultaneously:

1. **Procore is the comp** (massive market validated, ripe for AI-native challenger)
2. **Why now** (AI maturity meets construction industry's regulatory inflection)
3. **Why us** (founder-as-domain-expert; technical execution proven; first paid GC by close)
4. **Why this raise** (specific use of funds toward Embedded Payments launch April 2027)

The deck is **18 slides for the room, 25 slides in the appendix.** Aim for the room at 12 minutes; appendix slides for follow-up due-diligence questions.

---

## Storyline (the 60-second version)

> Procore is a $10B company built on a workflow problem from 2003. The 28-year-old PE running a $50M GC project today copies data between Procore, QuickBooks, Excel, and her phone. She drafts the same RFI follow-up three times a week. She has no leverage when a sub disputes a pay app.
>
> SiteSync is the AI superintendent that drafts those RFIs, drafts those daily logs, drafts those pay apps before she gets to her desk. Every action is hash-chain audited — court-defensible. Subs get a free portal — network effect. When a GC approves a pay app, money pushes to the sub the same business day with the lien waiver auto-attached.
>
> One paid GC by August. Three reference customers by October. Embedded Payments live April 2027. The category resets that day.
>
> $5M, 18 months runway, three engineers + a GTM lead. We're 90% of the way to the first paid contract on $0 of outside capital. With $5M we're 90% of the way to ten paid GCs and a Series A by Q4 2027.

## Brand + Visual Identity

The deck should look like the rest of SiteSync's "lethal calm" aesthetic:

- **Typography:** Inter or Söhne (workhorse, no novelty)
- **Color palette:** deep slate (#1a1f2e), iris gold (#FFB347 — the obvious Iris callback), construction safety orange (#FF6B35) for warnings only, slate gray (#6c757d), white space generous
- **Photography:** real construction sites, real PMs (with permission), no stock
- **Charts/diagrams:** flat, geometric, no 3D, no gradient
- **Logo placement:** small, footer, every slide
- **Footer:** "SiteSync — confidential — [date]" + page number

**No emojis. No exclamation points. No "we believe." No "world-class." No SaaS jargon.**
