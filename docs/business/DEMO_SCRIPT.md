# Demo Script

A 25-minute guided demo that takes a prospect from "what's wrong with Procore" to "I want this." The script is structured around the verbs in [docs/THE_FIVE.md](../THE_FIVE.md) and centers a single live story: a project where a pay app would have been rejected, and SiteSync caught it before submission.

## Pre-demo setup

- Use the demo seed in [scripts/seed-demo-account.ts](../../scripts/seed-demo-account.ts) (run via `npm run seed:demo-account`).
- Confirm Iris seeds via [scripts/seed-iris-inbox.ts](../../scripts/seed-iris-inbox.ts) (run via `npm run seed:iris-inbox`).
- Open two windows: the GC's view (PM role) and the Owner's view (read-only via magic link).

## Act 1 — The Day (3 min)

**The pitch:** *Procore makes you navigate by database table. SiteSync makes you navigate by what you're doing right now.*

1. Sign in as a superintendent.
2. Land on the dashboard at [src/pages/dashboard/index.tsx](../../src/pages/dashboard/index.tsx). Show today's daily log, the crew check-ins, the open punch items.
3. Hit Capture. Speak: *"Drywall in suite 312 has a finish defect on the south wall."* Snap a photo.
4. Show the structurer at [supabase/functions/structure-field-note/index.ts](../../supabase/functions/structure-field-note/index.ts) routing it to a punch item draft. The super never opened `/punch-list`.

## Act 2 — The Field (4 min)

**The pitch:** *The daily log writes itself.*

1. Open the daily log at [src/pages/daily-log/index.tsx](../../src/pages/daily-log/index.tsx).
2. Show the auto-drafted weather + manpower + section prose generated by [src/lib/dailyLogDrafting/index.ts](../../src/lib/dailyLogDrafting/index.ts).
3. Sign the log. The hash is stored per [src/lib/dailyLog/_hash.ts](../../src/lib/dailyLog/_hash.ts) — a tampered copy is provably altered.
4. Show that an edit creates a revision per [supabase/migrations/20260501110001_daily_log_revisions.sql](../../supabase/migrations/20260501110001_daily_log_revisions.sql), preserving the signed original.

## Act 3 — The Conversation (5 min)

**The pitch:** *One inbox, ball-in-court SLA, drafted responses.*

1. Switch to the PM view. Open The Conversation.
2. Show three Iris suggestions per [src/lib/iris/suggestPolicy.ts](../../src/lib/iris/suggestPolicy.ts) — an RFI > 5 days, a punch item > 7 days, a CO over $50k without a quote.
3. Click "Iris drafted a response" on the RFI. Show the gate in [src/components/iris/IrisApprovalGate.tsx](../../src/components/iris/IrisApprovalGate.tsx) — Iris drafts; humans approve.
4. Approve. The response is sent. The audit log captures the entire chain — Iris's draft, the PM's approval, the dispatch — per [docs/HASH_CHAIN_INVARIANTS.md](../HASH_CHAIN_INVARIANTS.md).

## Act 4 — The Set (3 min)

**The pitch:** *Drawings are the source of truth, and every photo links to a pin.*

1. Open the drawings module.
2. Show the photo-to-pin resolver at [src/lib/linkage/photoLinker.ts](../../src/lib/linkage/photoLinker.ts) — a captured photo from Act 1 is automatically linked to the suite-312 drawing pin.
3. Side-by-side revision compare (recently shipped per the git history) shows what changed between drawing revs.

## Act 5 — The Site (4 min)

**The pitch:** *Pay app night doesn't have to be a fire drill.*

1. Open the pay-app detail at [src/pages/payment-applications/PayAppDetail.tsx](../../src/pages/payment-applications/PayAppDetail.tsx).
2. Click "Pre-submission audit." The component is [src/pages/payment-applications/PreSubmissionAudit.tsx](../../src/pages/payment-applications/PreSubmissionAudit.tsx); the rules are [src/pages/payment-applications/auditChecks.ts](../../src/pages/payment-applications/auditChecks.ts).
3. Show two failures: a sub missing a lien waiver, and a sub whose COI expired mid-period.
4. Click each Fix link. Show the COI gate at [src/components/insurance/CoiBlockBanner.tsx](../../src/components/insurance/CoiBlockBanner.tsx) and the gate logic at [src/lib/coi/expirationGate.ts](../../src/lib/coi/expirationGate.ts).
5. Upload the missing waiver. The five checks now pass. Submit.
6. The server-side mirror at [supabase/functions/payapp-audit/index.ts](../../supabase/functions/payapp-audit/index.ts) is the authoritative gate — even with a forged client, the submission fails without all five passing or an override row.

## Act 6 — Owner share (2 min)

**The pitch:** *The owner doesn't need an account to review the pay app.*

1. Click "Share with owner." A magic link is generated per [supabase/migrations/20260501100000_magic_link_tokens.sql](../../supabase/migrations/20260501100000_magic_link_tokens.sql).
2. Open the link in the second window. Show [src/pages/share/OwnerPayAppPreview.tsx](../../src/pages/share/OwnerPayAppPreview.tsx) — read-only G702/G703, reconciliation snapshot, comments.
3. Token validation lives in [supabase/functions/owner-payapp-preview/index.ts](../../supabase/functions/owner-payapp-preview/index.ts).

## Act 7 — Portfolio (2 min)

**The pitch:** *Risk-rank every project in one screen.*

1. Switch to an executive view.
2. Open [src/pages/portfolio/PortfolioDashboard.tsx](../../src/pages/portfolio/PortfolioDashboard.tsx). Show the KPI tiles and the risk-ranked table.
3. The risk weighting is documented in [src/lib/portfolio/riskRanker.ts](../../src/lib/portfolio/riskRanker.ts).
4. Show cross-project search at [src/pages/portfolio/CrossProjectSearch.tsx](../../src/pages/portfolio/CrossProjectSearch.tsx); RLS is enforced server-side via [supabase/migrations/20260502120005_org_search_index.sql](../../supabase/migrations/20260502120005_org_search_index.sql).

## Act 8 — Procore migration (2 min)

**The pitch:** *Switching cost is zero. We import.*

1. Open [src/pages/admin/procore-import](../../src/pages/admin/procore-import).
2. Show the live import progress poller [src/pages/admin/procore-import/JobProgressView.tsx](../../src/pages/admin/procore-import/JobProgressView.tsx).
3. Provenance: every imported row carries `external_ids.procore_id` per [supabase/migrations/20260502120000_external_ids.sql](../../supabase/migrations/20260502120000_external_ids.sql) and the verbatim source in `legacy_payload` per [supabase/migrations/20260502120001_legacy_payload.sql](../../supabase/migrations/20260502120001_legacy_payload.sql).

## Close (30 sec)

*"Most platforms ask you to manage data. SiteSync asks: what are you doing right now? Then it does the data work."*

## Demo guardrails

- Do NOT promote stub features — see [HONEST_STATE.md](../../HONEST_STATE.md) for what is genuinely shipped vs aspirational.
- Do NOT quote prices — see [PRICING.md](PRICING.md). Prices are TBD; refer questions to sales.
- Do NOT claim SOC 2 certification. We have controls aligned with SOC 2; certification requires an actual auditor visit per [compliance/SOC2_EVIDENCE_PACK.md](../compliance/SOC2_EVIDENCE_PACK.md).
- The pre-submission audit must be mounted in PayAppDetail.tsx for Act 5 to work in a single click. Until that wiring lands per [STATUS.md](../STATUS.md), the demo path uses the audit page directly.
